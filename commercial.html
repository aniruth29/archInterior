JB Khanna Prints Showroom

square_foot1,700 sq.ft. storefrontShowroom location_onEgmore, Chennai

The 1,700 sq.ft. JB Khanna Prints showroom in Egmore, Chennai, showcases a sophisticated integration of retail display and administrative functionality. The design optimizes high-density frame display systems and integrated storage solutions, tailored specifically for large-scale print collections. The spatial layout features a premium MD suite and dedicated workstations, unified by sleek glass partitions and a custom false ceiling with precision lighting. This project achieves a seamless transition between a vibrant gallery and a professional office environment, utilizing a modern display counter as the central focal point of the interior.

TVS Brakes India Corporate Office

square_foot48,000 sq.ft. corporate_fareCorporate Office location_onJhagadia, Gujarat

The 48,000-square-foot corporate office for TVS Brakes India in Jhagadia, Gujarat, represents a significant administrative expansion integrated within their industrial facility. This modern workspace is characterized by a sophisticated architectural design, utilizing extensive elevation glazing and glass partitions to maximize natural light across multiple levels. The interior features high-grade materials, including wooden and grid ceilings, alongside a combination of wooden and vinyl flooring to define functional zones like open-plan workstations, executive cabins, a pantry, and a conference area. This development provides a high-end, collaborative environment for the company's engineering and administrative teams, supporting its large-scale manufacturing operations in the region.
