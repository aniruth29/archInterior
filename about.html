About Us

Creating lasting impressions through exceptional interior design and unparalleled craftsmanship.

At Arch Interior, we believe great design is a powerful blend of functionality, elegance, and personal expression.

Based in Chennai, we specialize in delivering immaculate interior solutions for modern living spaces and professional work environments. We operate with an in-house factory ensuring top-tier quality control from conceptualization to the final build.

  • Layout planning
  • 3D visualization
  • Material selection
  • Furniture making
  • Final installation

We are a Chennai-based interior design firm with our head office located in Ambattur.

In addition to our office, we operate a fully equipped aluminium fabrication unit and a wood processing unit in Chennai, allowing us to maintain complete control over quality, precision, and project timelines.

We undertake residential and commercial interior projects across Chennai and its surrounding areas. Our experience also extends beyond the city, having successfully executed commercial projects across India in collaboration with architects and industry professionals.

With strong in-house manufacturing capabilities, we provide end-to-end interior solutions — from planning and material selection to fabrication and final execution. This integrated approach ensures consistent quality, customization, and timely delivery for every project.

We believe our work speaks for itself. The trust of our clients is reflected in the long-term relationships we build, with many returning to us for their future projects.

Our commitment: quality craftsmanship, practical design solutions, and spaces that add lasting value.
